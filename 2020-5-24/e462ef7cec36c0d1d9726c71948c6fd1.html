COVID-19 Cases in Kansas

Map Information

Beginning May 11, the map is updated Monday, Wednesday and Friday by 12:30 p.m. The map contains a variety of data at the state and county level, including the Reopen Kansas Metrics. To maximize your experience, use a device with a larger screen, such as a tablet, laptop or desktop. The Map FAQ Document (PDF) includes detailed instructions on how to use and share the data.
